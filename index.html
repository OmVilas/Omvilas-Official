Our Story

Traditional Taste, Modern Quality

Founded in 1998, Om Vilas Condiments began as a small family kitchen in Mandya, where Mr. C. Chinnasamy would prepare traditional snacks for friends and family. Word of her delicious recipes spread, and what started as a passion project grew into a beloved brand.

Today, we continue to use the same authentic recipes, combining them with modern quality standards to bring you the best of Indian snacks. Each product is made with care, using only the finest ingredients and traditional methods.

Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a trusted name in Indian condiments, with customers across the country and beyond.
